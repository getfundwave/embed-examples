Fundwave Embed Button

Embed Button


Use Fundwave embed button on your own website to give your investors a direct access to the Fundwave Investor Portal.



How To Use


  • Select one of the themes - light or dark.
  • Copy the embed code on your website.
  • Replace 'app.getfundwave.com' with your instance name, for example - 'futurevision.getfundwave.com'
